What is Cavity Wall Insulation?

Cavity wall insulation involves filling the gap (or cavity) between the inner and outer walls of your home with insulating material. This reduces heat loss, making your home warmer in winter and cooler in summer.

Key Features of Cavity Wall Insulation:

  • Prevents up to 35% of heat loss through walls.

  • Reduces energy consumption and heating bills.

  • Improves indoor comfort by maintaining consistent temperatures.

  • Helps reduce condensation and dampness issues.

Benefits of Cavity Wall Insulation

1. Lower Energy Bills

Cavity wall insulation reduces heat loss, meaning you'll use less energy to keep your home warm, saving you hundreds of pounds annually on heating costs.

2. Improved Comfort

Proper insulation ensures a consistent indoor temperature, eliminating cold drafts and creating a cozy living environment year-round.

3. Reduced Carbon Footprint

By using less energy for heating, cavity wall insulation helps lower greenhouse gas emissions, contributing to a greener planet.

4. Increased Property Value

Homes with good insulation are more appealing to buyers and renters, boosting your property's market value.

5. Affordable Upgrades

With the ECO4 grant, eligible households can access cavity wall insulation at little or no cost, making it an affordable solution for improving energy efficiency.

What is the ECO4 Grant?

The ECO4 grant is part of the UK government's Energy Company Obligation (ECO) scheme aimed at reducing fuel poverty and improving energy efficiency in low-income households. This scheme provides funding for various energy-saving measures, including cavity wall insulation, at no cost to eligible applicants.

Key Features of the ECO4 Grant:

  • Targets low-income households and those in fuel poverty.

  • Covers a wide range of energy-saving measures like insulation and heating upgrades.

  • Runs until March 2026 with £4 billion in funding allocated for improvements across the UK.

Eligibility Criteria for Cavity Wall Insulation Under ECO4 Grant

To qualify for cavity wall insulation under the ECO4 grant, you must meet specific requirements:

  1. Income-Based Benefits:

    • Universal Credit

    • Pension Credit Guarantee

    • Income Support

    • Working Tax Credit or Child Tax Credit

  2. Property Type:

    • Homes with uninsulated cavity walls or poorly insulated walls are prioritized.

  3. Energy Performance Certificate (EPC):

    • Properties with an EPC rating of D, E, F, or G are eligible.

  4. Local Authority Flexibility (ECO Flex):

    • If you don't receive benefits but are considered vulnerable or on a low income (e.g., due to health conditions), you may still qualify under local authority discretion.
